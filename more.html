More about Manas

My journey in the field of Computer Science starts from an Inter-School Computer Science Competition in Grade 4 at my school. I joined VIT Vellore in 2019 to major in Computer Science and opted for a specialization in Business Systems. This is when my journey started in its true sense. Although C++ was my first language and I could fluently code in it, I started studying Python with the help of Coursera through a course from University of Michigan and within one year I went from learning python to coding complex Machine Learning applications.

I invested my time in building my skillset as fast as possible but as efficiently as necessary and recieved certifications in the following:

  • Machine Learning with Python
  • Data Visualization with Python
  • Exploratory Data Analysis with Python
  • Python for Data Science and AI
  • Databases and SQL for Data Science
  • Data Science Methodology
  • Open Source Tools for Data Science
  • IBM Data Science Professional Certificate
  • Fundamentals of AWS for Machine Learning

Along with these, I significantly invested my time in learning from a diverse set of resources from various top tier educational institutions like Udacity, Coursera, University of Waterloo, University of London and industrial institutions like IBM, Amazon etc


Apart from gaining skills, I enjoy participating in hackathons and mentoring fellow developers to efficiently build and deploy AI models. I have mentored in the following Hackathons:

  • Shell Hacks by Florida International University
  • Hack The North by University of Waterloo
  • Rotathon by Delhi Technological University

My aim is to build truly intelligent applications and replicate human consciousness, consequently contribute to the future of AI. I still believe we are yet to see its true potential and we are (at least theoretically) scratching the surface of an entire universe in the field of Computing and Mathematics.
